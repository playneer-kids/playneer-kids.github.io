Privacy Policy for Playneer Kids

Last updated: 2026-01-14

At Playneer Kids (accessible from https://playneer-kids.github.io/), one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document applies to all tools and games hosted on this platform, including Dictation Tool (默書神器) and Type Master Global (打字遊戲).

Collection of Data

Dictation Tool: We do NOT collect, store, or upload any text you input into the Dictation Tool. All processing (speech synthesis and text analysis) happens locally on your device (Client-side processing).

Type Master Global: Game scores are stored locally in your browser's "Local Storage" and are not uploaded to any server. We do not collect personal identifiable information.

Log Files

Playneer Kids follows a standard procedure of using log files provided by hosting services (e.g., GitHub Pages). The information collected includes IP addresses, browser type, ISP, date/time stamp, and referring/exit pages.

Cookies and Advertising

We use Google AdSense to serve ads. Google uses cookies (DART cookies) to serve ads based on your visit to this site and other sites on the Internet.

You may choose to decline the use of DART cookies by visiting the Google ad and content network Privacy Policy at: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

Children's Information

We encourage parents and guardians to observe and guide their children's online activity. Playneer Kids does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13. If you believe your child provided this kind of information on our website, please contact us immediately.

Contact Us

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at playneer.official@gmail.com.
